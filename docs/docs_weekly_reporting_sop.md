# Weekly Reporting SOP

## Schedule
- Monday 10:00 local: export platform reports for prior week (Mon–Sun)
- Monday 12:00: publish weekly summary

## Required exports (CSV)
- spend by day by campaign
- clicks & impressions by day by campaign
- conversions & revenue by day by campaign
- (optional) CRM funnel by campaign (leads, MQL, SQL)

## Core KPIs
- Spend
- CTR = clicks / impressions
- CPC = spend / clicks
- CVR = conversions / clicks
- CAC (proxy) = spend / conversions
- ROAS = revenue / spend

## Minimum analysis
1. Week-over-week deltas for Spend, Conversions, CAC, ROAS
2. Top movers (best/worst) by campaign for CAC and Conversions
3. 3 bullet “drivers” (what changed and why) with evidence

## Notes
Do not attribute causality without evidence. If the driver is unknown, list hypotheses and next checks.
